Job Polska Stand




Bielsko-Biala, Poland, 2010
by Lukasz Gawlas & Krzysztof Leszczynski

Job Fair Stand Design for Job Polska



Client, job recruitment agency, was in need to prepare promotional design operating on outgoing events like job fairs and promotional displays. Aim of this design was mobile, multifunctional furniture able to support that kind of events. Created box acts as poster pillar, laptop table and closet for promotional materials or workers luggage. With geometry based on triangular faces, box has structural skin which canceled separated framing system. Stand is easy to disassemble into two parts, what simplify transport.
