Background



GL05 started in 2005 as collaboration of two architects, Lukasz Gawlas and Krzysztof Leszczynski, set up for entering several architectural and design competitions.

Presently we run architecture studio operating on fields of design, architecture and urban planing.

We deal with projects from its conceptual stage, through planing and building permit, ending with execution and detailed design. As general designer we coordinate and cooperate with technical discipline engineers.


Work



We are aware of architecture ubiquity in every man’s life and its power of creating solutions.
We use individual approach against each project, to create unique response.
We perceive architecture as a discipline interfering with many others and interdisciplinary at presumption.
While we respect architecture history, there’s no need for going into slavery of the past.
We go after applying of contemporary world technical knowledge and experience.
